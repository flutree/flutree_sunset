Sunsetting Flutree

Flutree has reached more than 50,000 installs, yet so close to its first anniversary, but the time has come to say goodbye.

Tonight (MYT), Flutree app(s) will no longer be available for downloads from the Google Play Store nor accessible on the flutree.studio site. Users no longer can create a new profile.

Meanwhile, flutree.web.app will continue serving visitors until 31 August 2021. Please migrate to another service for your convenience.

Urmm, why?

Flutree is no longer generating enough revenue to support the costs of maintaining it.

We received a tremendous number of visitors that makes the hosting usages went beyond the roof. In addition, there is a sudden decline in the ads impressions with minimal chance to recover.

So, what's next?

Move on and (perhaps) come up with a better business model. Until then, sayonana <3
