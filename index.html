Sunsetting Flutree

Flutree has reached 50,000 installs, yet close to celebrating the anniversary date, but the time has come to finally say goodbye.

It is a difficult decision to make. On midnight, 24 August 2021 (Malaysian Time), Flutree's mobile and web will no longer available for downloads from the Google Play Store nor accessible on the flutree.studio site. That means users no longer can create a new profile.

Meanwhile, flutree.web.app will continue serving visitors until 31 August 2021. Please migrate to another service for your convenience.

Why are you doing this?

Flutree is no longer generating enough revenue to support the costs of maintaining it.

We received a tremendous number of visitors that makes the hosting costs went beyond the roof. In addition, we encountered a sudden drops of the ads impressions.

So, what's next?

Move on and (perhaps) reconstruct a better business model. Until then, sayonana <3
