Flutree has reached 50,000 installs, yet close to celebrating the anniversary date, but the time has come to finally say goodbye.

It is a difficult decision to make. Tonight, Flutree's mobile and web will no longer be available for download in the Google Play Store or accessible on the flutree.studio site. That means users no longer can create a new profile or update the existing one.

Meanwhile, flutree.web.app will continue serving visitors until 31 August 2021. Please migrate to another service to avoid any inconvenience.

Why are you doing this?

Flutree is no longer generating enough revenue to support the costs of maintaining it.

We received a tremendous number of visitors that makes the hosting costs went beyond the roof. On the other hand, we didn't gain enough ads impressions from the app.

So, what's next?

Move on and (perhaps) reconstruct a better business model.
